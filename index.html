About me

Originally from France, I graduated with a bachelor of Laws degree, then moved to the United States.
I had a career break while being a stay at home mother and later I re-entered the workforce starting a career in Real Estate.
I quickly realized that tech-brokerages were disrupting the industry. This piqued my interest and I began self learning to code.
To go further and improve my knowledge, I joined and graduated from a full stack Software Engineer bootcamp.
I found my real passion in the creation of customer centric applications that solve individual and company problems. Below are some of the projects I built during my Bootcamp and beyond. I created a Customer Relationship management application because of the need I had for such a tool as a new Real Estate agent. I used different languages and frameworks to showcase my understanding of their functional pros and cons. My final goal is to deploy a fully functioning free app available for new Real Estate agents.
